
"Anyway," he goes on, "Your tongue has been removed to prevent you
from trying to tell any of your fellow laborers about the fact that no
one in society knows they exist. You will be watched, and if you look
like you're even thinking about trying to communicate what you know to
the native laborers, you will be punished. Nor may you attempt to
teach them to read or write."

"Do I make myself clear?" he asks.

Unable to do anything else, you nod.
